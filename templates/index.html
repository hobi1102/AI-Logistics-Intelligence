Predictive models for freight cost and invoice risk analysis.

How to Use & How It Works

Usage: Input your vendor invoice or purchase order details into the forms below and click the button to generate an instant prediction.

Under the Hood: This website is powered by a Flask Python backend. It runs your data through two Random Forest Machine Learning models that were trained on your local inventory database using Scikit-Learn:

  • Freight Predictor: Uses the Invoice Dollars feature to continuously predict the estimated freight cost.
  • Invoice Risk: Uses a scaled combination of Invoice Qty, Invoice $, Freight, PO Total Qty, and PO Total Dollars to classify if an invoice should be flagged as risky or anomalous.
